This is a blog where trans people of color can submit selfies and other content! Anyone can follow! Thank you for your support :)

Whether you identify as a BIAPIM (Black, Indigenous, Asian, Pacific Islander, or mixed) trans woman, trans man, or non-binary person, you're welcome to submit as long as you identify as non-cisgender and non-white.

transgenderteensurvivalguide:

Lee says:

Here’s a transitioning starter pack for all my trans folk out there! 

Your transition is up to you- we want to provide information on all your options so you can make an informed decision about what you want. If you find a resource useful, then use it! If you’re not interested in doing any of the things listed, then don’t!
