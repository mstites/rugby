About Me

This site was created by non-binary, poly, lesbian Maeve Stites. They created this site to display and document their creative side projects. Create is an ongoing project they plan to continue updating for the foreseeable future.

They are an Electrical and Computer Engineering major at Olin College of Engineering with special interests in robotics, programming, and engineering for social good. In addition to engineering, they have a passion for social justice and diversity, equity, and inclusion work.

Outside of her work, they enjoy a variety of hobbies including writing zines, Linux shenanigans, custom computer keyboards, fashion, and rugby.
